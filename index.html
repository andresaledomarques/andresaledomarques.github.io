Andresa Lêdo Marques

Postdoctoral Researcher · Institute of Social Sciences, University of Lisbon

Andresa Lêdo Marques is a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Social Sciences, University of Lisbon, working on the research project NATURESCAPES (nature-based solutions for climate-resilient, nature-positive and socially just communities). Her research focuses on urban and regional planning, climate adaptation, multi-scalar planning, and nature-based solutions.

She holds a cotutelle PhD (2024) from Mackenzie Presbyterian University (Brazil) and Leibniz University Hannover (Germany), and an MSc (2019) and BArch (2017) from Mackenzie.
